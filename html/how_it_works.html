The process

Here's what working together actually looks like.

No mystery. No complicated funnels. Just a clear process that's worked for over 100 busy professionals — and adapts to fit your life, not the other way around.

Book Your Free Strategy Call See client results

From first conversation to consistent results, here's exactly how this works.

01
Phase One

First, we talk.

Before anything else, we have a conversation. This is a free 25–30 minute call where I learn about your situation and you learn how I work. No pressure. No pitch. Just clarity.

What I'll ask you

  • What's your goal — and why does it matter to you right now?
  • What does your typical week actually look like?
  • What have you tried before? What worked? What didn't?
  • What equipment do you have access to — if any?
  • What's the biggest thing that's gotten in your way?

What you'll learn

  • How I'd approach your specific situation
  • What a realistic timeline looks like for your goal
  • Exactly what's included — and what's expected of you
  • Whether we're a good fit (not everyone is, and that's fine)

By the end of the call: You'll know exactly what working together would look like. If it makes sense for both of us, I'll invite you to start. If not, you'll leave with more clarity than you came with.

Format: Video call (Google Meet or Zoom) or phone — your choice.

02
Phase Two

Then, I build your programme.

Within 3–5 days of starting, you'll receive a complete training and nutrition programme designed specifically for you. Not a template I've sent to 500 other people. A programme built from our conversation, your constraints, and your goals.

Training programme

Your workouts structured by week — exercise selection, sets, reps, rest times, and progression built in. Designed around your available equipment and realistic schedule.

Nutrition guidance

Not a rigid meal plan you'll abandon in a week. Practical guidance tailored to your preferences, your local food options, and your lifestyle. Clear targets. Flexible execution.

Programme document

Everything delivered in a clean, accessible format you can pull up on your phone. No complicated apps to learn. No subscription required.

2

What I ask of you

This is a partnership.
Here's your side of it.

I'm selective about who I work with. Not because I want to seem exclusive — but because coaching only works when both sides show up. I bring the expertise, the structure, and the accountability. You bring the effort and the honesty.

1
Complete your check-ins weekly.

Takes 5–10 minutes. This is how I know what's working and what needs to change. If you go silent, I can't help you.

2
Follow the programme at least 80% of the time.

Perfection isn't the goal. Consistency is. Life will interrupt. That's fine. But if you're only training once a week, we don't have enough to work with.

3
Communicate when life happens.

Travelling? Tell me. Injured? Tell me. Work exploded? Tell me. I can adjust for almost anything — but only if I know about it.

4
Be honest.

About what you're actually doing. About what's hard. About what's not working. I'm not here to judge you. I'm here to help you. But I can only help with the truth.

If that sounds like you, we'll work well together.
